Berbere

158 ETB

The foundation of Ethiopian cuisine. A robust, complex, and fiery blend of sun-dried red chilies, garlic, ginger, basil, korarima, rue, ajwain or radhuni, nigella, and fenugreek.

Chili peppers, garlic, ginger, basil, korarima, rue, ajwain, nigella, and fenugreek. All 100% natural and sun-dried.

Essential for Doro Wat (chicken stew), Misir Wat (lentil stew), and as a dry rub for roasted meats.
