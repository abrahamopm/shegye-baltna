Our Story

Shegye is West baltna... We bring you the authentic taste of Ethiopia, sourced directly from the finest farmers and prepared with generations of care.

"A tradition of flavor, preserved for your table."

Our spices are more than ingredients; they are stories of our land, sun-dried and perfectly blended.

The Shegye Process

1. The Sourcing

We work hand-in-hand with local farming cooperatives across the Ethiopian highlands. By ensuring fair trade, we secure the most vibrant, robust chilis and spices right after harvest.

2. The Preparation

Patience is our secret ingredient. Our spices are slowly sun-dried to naturally intensify their flavor profiles, preserving the essential oils that give Ethiopian cuisine its distinct aroma.
